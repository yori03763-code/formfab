### Direct Competitors

#### 1. Shapeways
**Strengths:**
- 60+ materials
- 10+ years market presence
- Strong B2B relationships
- Global manufacturing network

**Weaknesses:**
- Single material per product
- No AI assistance
- No AR preview
- Complex pricing
- Poor mobile UX

**Pricing:**
- White Plastic: ~$0.15/cm³
- Metals: $1.50-3.00/cm³

**Strengths:**

**Weaknesses:**

- Basic UI

**Weaknesses:**

## BUSINESS RISKS & MITIGATION

### Risk 1: Manufacturing Partner Dependency
**Risk:** Shapeways API changes, outages, pricing changes
**Mitigation:**
- Multi-vendor strategy (add Sculpteo, Xometry)
- Build direct manufacturer relationships
- Consider in-house printing for high-volume

### Risk 2: AI Generation Quality
**Risk:** Meshy generates poor models
**Mitigation:**
- Allow regeneration
- Manual refinement tools
- Quality check before print

### Risk 3: Market Education
**Risk:** Users don't understand multi-material value
**Mitigation:**
- Clear examples
- Case studies
- Tutorial content

### Risk 4: Payment Fraud
**Risk:** Chargebacks, fraudulent orders
**Mitigation:**
- Stripe Radar
- Manual review for high-value
- Clear policies

### Risk 5: Competitor Copy
**Risk:** Shapeways adds multi-material
**Mitigation:**
- First-mover advantage
- Build brand and community
- Continuous innovation
